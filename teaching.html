SI 552: Introduction to Accessibility

This course is for Master's students enrolled at the University of Michigan. It is an introduction to accessibility. Students engage in discourse on several models of disability, are exposed to different types of assistive technologies, and apply what they learn to real-world accessibility and technology challenges.

Contact Dr. Brewer if interested in a copy of the syllabus for this course
